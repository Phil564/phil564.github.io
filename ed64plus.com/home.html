What is ED64Plus?  ED64Plus = Rom Cartridge Emulator, Flash Card and Backup Card for N64 system



How to use :

1. Copy your Rom files onto the SD card , put your SD into ED64Plus Cartridge.



2. Insert your Rom cartridge into your N64 console.


3. Play the loaded game from your ED64Plus with your N64.....
